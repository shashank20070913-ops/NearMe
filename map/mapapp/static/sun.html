To describe a sunset, focus on vivid colors (fiery reds, golden hues, purples), the sun's descent (sinking, melting, dipping), the mood (peaceful, dramatic, serene), sensory details (cool air, birds returning), and poetic metaphors (sky as a canvas, molten orb) to paint a rich picture beyond just "orange and red". Engage senses, use strong verbs and adjectives, and highlight unique elements like cloud colors or reflections to make it memorable.
